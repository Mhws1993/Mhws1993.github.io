Simple. Clean. Accessible.

I am Matthew Smith.

I am a geographer and a cartographer. But first and foremost I am a storyteller.

I believe in making things that are simple and accessible, whether it be a map or a research paper.

I've been lucky enough to travel the world. I've gotten to experience things that few others have been lucky enough to experience.

I believe everyone and everything has a story and that everyone's story is worth hearing.

There needs to be a story woven into any map or piece of writing to taken them on a journey.

My design philosophy

My design philsophy is simple. Clean. Simple. Accessible.

I believe that, whether you're making a map or writing up a report, it needs to be clean, simple and acessible.

This means that the visuals need to be cleaned up and presented in the best light. There needs to be a balance between how much information you have and how much you need to give. Sometimes less is more. Finally the product you are presenting needs to be accessible to the audience that wants to see the product. The audience needs to be able to understand what you are showing them and so the product needs to be presented in a way that is easy to understand.
